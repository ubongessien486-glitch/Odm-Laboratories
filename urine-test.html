URINE TESTING

$65.00 – $105.00

Comprehensive Urine Analysis Panels

Urine testing remains the most common and standardized method for drug screening due to its reliability and detection window. We provide thorough collection and analysis for both regulated and non-regulated testing needs.

Our certified collectors strictly follow all chain-of-custody protocols to ensure specimen integrity and accurate laboratory reporting.

Available Testing Panels:

  • 5-Panel Urine Test ($65.00): Tests for Amphetamines, Cocaine, Marijuana, Opiates, and PCP. (Standard DOT requirement)
  • 10-Panel Urine Test ($85.00): Tests for 5-panel drugs plus Barbiturates, Benzodiazepines, Methadone, Methaqualone, and Propoxyphene.
  • 13-Panel Urine Test ($105.00): Comprehensive testing covering 10-panel drugs plus additional prescription medications and synthetics.
