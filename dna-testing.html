Confidential & Highly Accurate DNA Analysis

We provide comprehensive DNA testing services for legal, medical, and personal requirements. Our tests are conducted using strict chain-of-custody protocols by certified collectors to ensure the results are legally admissible and 99.99% accurate.

Types of DNA Tests We Offer:

  • Paternity Testing: Establish biological relationships definitively. Available for both legal and personal knowledge.
  • Non-Invasive Prenatal Paternity: Safe testing available early in pregnancy using maternal blood.
  • Immigration DNA Testing: AABB-accredited testing for visa and immigration requirements.
  • Siblingship / Grandparentage: Determine biological relationships when parents are unavailable for testing.
